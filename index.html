About Me

Hello! I'm Dmitry Pavlov, a passionate seminary student with deep interests in both spiritual studies and mathematical theories. Currently residing in the historic town of Pechory in Russia's Pskov region, I find joy in exploring the connections between faith, numbers, and traditional healing practices.

Beyond my academic pursuits, I'm an avid watercolor painter, capturing the beauty of the world through colors and brushstrokes. Music is another passion of mine, with The Beatles and Os Mutantes being among my favorite bands that inspire both my artistic and spiritual journey.

My Interests

My intellectual and creative pursuits span across several fascinating domains:

Number Theory Chinese Acupuncture Theology Mathematics Traditional Medicine Watercolor Painting The Beatles Os Mutantes Music Art

I'm particularly fascinated by number theory - the study of integers and their properties. The beauty of prime numbers and mathematical patterns captivates my analytical mind.

Parallel to this, I've developed a profound interest in Chinese acupuncture, exploring how this ancient healing art balances energy flows in the human body. The precision and philosophy behind acupuncture resonate with my mathematical mindset.

In my creative time, I express myself through watercolor paintings, often while listening to the timeless music of The Beatles or the psychedelic sounds of Os Mutantes, which provide endless inspiration.

Current Focus

As I progress through my seminary education, I'm continuously seeking ways to integrate my diverse interests. I believe there are meaningful connections to be discovered between numerical patterns, spiritual concepts, and holistic healing approaches.

When I'm not engaged in theological studies, you might find me solving complex mathematical problems, studying meridians and acupuncture points in traditional Chinese medicine, or creating new watercolor artworks inspired by both my spiritual journey and favorite music.
